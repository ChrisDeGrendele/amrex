10 /*
11  A FabSet is a group of FArrayBox's. The grouping is designed
12  specifically to represent regions along the boundary of Box's,
13  and are used to implement boundary conditions to discretized
14  partial differential equations.
15 
16  A FabSet is an array of pointers to FABs. The standard FAB operators,
17  however, have been modified to be more useful for maintaining
18  boundary conditions for partial differential equations discretized
19  on boxes.
20  Under normal circumstances, a FAB will be created for each face of a
21  box. For a group of boxes, a FabSet will be the group of FABs at a
22  particular orientation (ie. the lo-i side of each grid in a list).
23 
24  Since a FabSet FAB will likely be used to bound a grid box,
25  FArrayBox::resize() operations are disallowed. Also, to preserve
26  flexibility in applicable boundary scenarios, intersecting
27  FABs in the FabSet are not guaranteed to contain identical data--thus
28  copy operations from a FabSet to any FAB-like structure may be
29  order-dependent.
30 
31  FabSets are used primarily as a data storage mechanism, and are
32  manipulated by more sophisticated control classes.
33 */
